Cookies

A cookie is saved information in a file on your computer or device that is used when you visit a web page. The cookie contains useful information about you as a visitor to the page that can be read next time you get to that web page. It may be information of want kind of search you did, if you bought something, things that you written in forms, your type of device or any other integrated cookie-collector from for example google, facebook or twitter.
